How to Choose a Steak: The FAQs

When choosing a steak to cook, you’ve got a lot of options to filter through. Prime or Select? Angus? Grass fed or grain fed? Dry aged or wet aged? Sirloin steak or T-bone steak? Which combination of the above should you get? An Angus, Prime, grass-fed, dry-aged T-bone steak? What does that even mean? Below we answer all the common questions that arise as you try to decide what steak you’re going to pick and cook.
